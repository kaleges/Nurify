Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

TL;DR — Nurify does not track you, does not sell your data, and does not show ads. Your worship data stays on your device. We only collect what Firebase Authentication requires (email and name) so you can sign in.

1. Who We Are

Nurify is developed and maintained by Kaleges ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains how Nurify (the "App") handles your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information (Firebase Authentication)

When you create an account or sign in, we collect:

  • Email address — to identify your account
  • Display name — to personalize your experience
  • User ID — a unique identifier generated by Firebase

This data is processed by Google Firebase and is used solely for authentication and app functionality.

2.2 Worship & App Data

Your prayer logs, dhikr counts, fasting records, Quran bookmarks, streaks, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not transmitted to any server.

2.3 Location (Optional)

If you grant permission, the App accesses your location only to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. Location data is processed on-device and is never sent to our servers.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising
  • We do not serve ads or use ad networks
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties
  • We do not create user profiles for marketing purposes

4. How We Use Your Information

The limited data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your account and enable sign-in
  • Provide core app features (prayer times, notifications)
  • Sync your preferences if you sign in on a new device

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Authentication data is stored securely by Firebase (Google Cloud infrastructure)
  • Local app data resides on your device and is protected by iOS encryption
  • We do not maintain separate servers or databases for user content

6. Third-Party Services

Nurify uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication — account management (Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — push notifications (Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase Firestore — cloud data sync (Privacy Policy)

7. Children's Privacy

Nurify does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is suitable for all ages, and parental guidance is recommended for younger users.

8. Your Rights

You can at any time:

  • Delete your account — through the App's Settings, which removes your Firebase authentication data
  • Delete local data — by uninstalling the App
  • Revoke location access — through iOS Settings
  • Request data export or deletion — by contacting us

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
